(KPL) As part of his official visit to Mongolia and participation in the 10th Foreign Ministers’ Meeting of the Forum for East Asia–Latin America Cooperation (FEALAC) on August 21, Foreign Minister of the Lao PDR Thongsavanh Phomvihane and his delegation paid a courtesy call on Mongolian Prime Minister Gombojav Zandanshatar at the State Palace in Ulaanbaatar.

Minister Thongsavanh expressed gratitude for the warm hospitality extended by the Mongolian government and conveyed cordial greetings and best wishes from Prime Minister Sonexay Siphandone and President of the National Assembly of Laos Xaysomphone Phomvihane to Prime Minister Zandanshatar. He also congratulated Mr. Zandanshatar on his election as Mongolia’s new Prime Minister in July 2025 and extended felicitations on the 819th National Day of Mongolia, celebrated on July 11, 2025.
